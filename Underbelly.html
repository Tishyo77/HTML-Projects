Welcome to Underbelly


Underbelly is located inside Vellore Institute of Technology (Bhopal) near the Academic Block. We serve a huge variety of meals, snacks and beverages to the students of the university and our prices are very affordable as well. We maintain hygiene since out top priority is providing safe food to the students. We offer free delivery to the hostels as well. We are open from 9:00 in the morning till 7:30 in the evening.

Address: Kothri Kalan Dist, Sehore Pin Code: 466114
